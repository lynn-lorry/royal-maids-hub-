What We Do

We recruit Domestic Workers (DWs) from all over the country approved by the Ministry of Manpower of Uganda (MOU).

We will assist employers in their DW selection process by giving honest feedback and advice, arrange interviews through phone or video calls, arrange all the documentation, and apply work permit for the selected DW.

We provide counseling and if necessary re-training to DWs who have difficulty fitting into the culture or the employer‚Äôs household.

We train these DWs in their respective regions and when they are ready, we match them with suitable families.

We will pick up the DW on her arrival date, bring her for a pre-employment medical check-up, send her for SIP (Settling-In Programme), and finally arrange for her thumb printing to be done.
